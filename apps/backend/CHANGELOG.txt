    app/db/client.py
    app/modules/auth/dto.py

  Changes:
    • New env vars: BREVO_API_KEY, EMAIL_FROM_ADDRESS, EMAIL_FROM_NAME,

    • New app/core/email.py — Brevo transactional email service with

    • AuthService.reset_password() — validates token (not used, not expired),
      updates password, marks token used
    • POST /auth/forgot-password (3/min) and POST /auth/reset-password (5/min)
